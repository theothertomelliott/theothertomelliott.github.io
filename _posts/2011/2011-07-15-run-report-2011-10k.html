Another British 10k has come and gone, and nearly a week later, it's probably time I updated you all on how it went!

Ok, so it wasn't my best year, and after a rather unfortunate stitch at the 7k mark, my final time was 1 hour and 2 minutes. Four minutes down on previous years, but it's still respectable. Plus it was a lot of fun! If you ignore the kilometer of side pain.

Over 25,000 people ran the race this year, and I was treated to such sights as Scooby Doo running on two lets, a Darth Vader/Stormtrooper duo, and no less than three people puking within 100m of the finish. Special mention to the determined guy who adopted a puke-run-puke-run strategy.
